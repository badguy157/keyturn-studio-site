Bitwarden Send (free & encrypted)

  1. Create a free account and sign in to the Bitwarden Web Vault.
  2. Go to Send → New → Text.
  3. Paste only what’s needed (use the template below).
  4. Click Options and set:
    • Expiration: 7 days
    • Limit views: 1
    • Password: add a simple passphrase — this locks the Bitwarden Send message, not your DNS account. Share this passphrase separately (text/phone/second email).
  5. Click Create, copy the link, and email it to hello@keyturn.studio.

Recipients don’t need a Bitwarden account to open your link. We open it once, store credentials in our encrypted vault, and confirm receipt.

We only use Bitwarden Send for domain / DNS and, if needed, hosting or analytics logins. We never ask for passwords to your booking system, OTAs (Booking.com, Expedia, etc.) or your Google listing.
