Project Goals

  • Lift website-direct booking share and reduce OTA commission
  • Clarify “Book direct — best rate” CTAs across pages
  • Add GA4 events to track booking-outbound clicks

Before → After

  • No sticky CTA → unified sticky bar + hero button
  • Slower hero → optimized images & layout polish
  • No GA4 events → booking_outbound & book_click
  • Unstructured FAQs → Hotel / Restaurant / FAQ JSON-LD

Early Metrics

  • Website-direct snapshot (off-peak): 18.8% (3 of 16)
  • Seasonally-weighted annual website-direct: 21.7%
  • Outbound booking CTR increased with sticky CTA

Off-peak snapshot: 18.8% (3/16). Annual weighting uses off-peak ≈9.8% of yearly bookings. In-season assumption: 22% website-direct.

Traffic → Bookings Impact

  • Clicks +107% (85 vs 41) after launch
  • CTR 5.1% vs 2.3%; average position 15.7 vs 23.7

Window: launch → yesterday vs prior period; mostly branded immediately post-launch (expected).
