How it works

Enter a few numbers about your hotel:

  • How many rooms you have
  • Your typical nightly rate
  • How busy you are across the year
  • Roughly how many bookings currently come from OTAs vs direct

We'll then estimate:

  • How much you're paying in OTA commission today
  • How much you could save each year
  • How many months it might take for a website project to pay for itself

It's not exact, but it's a good starting point for a decision.

How to read these numbers

Treat this as a guide, not a promise. Real-world results depend on many things: your photos, your reviews, your pricing, and how strongly we push direct bookings on your site.

As a simple rule of thumb:

  • If the payback period is under 18 months, most owners feel comfortable going ahead.
  • If it's longer, it may still be worth doing if your site is very out of date or you have other reasons to refresh it.

Common questions

What if I don't know my exact occupancy?

Use your best guess, or just pick a rough figure. You can always share your real numbers later when you request a quote.

Does this include extra bookings from a nicer website?

No, this calculator only looks at commission savings from moving bookings from OTAs to direct. A better site can also increase overall bookings, but we'd rather keep this tool conservative.

Can you help me work out the numbers properly?

Yes. Send us your details through the quote form and we'll walk through it with you.
