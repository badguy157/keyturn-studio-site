Common questions

Do I have to learn any new systems?

No. We work with your existing booking system and domain provider wherever possible. We'll ask for access once at the start, then handle the setup for you.

Can you work with my current booking engine?

In most cases, yes. As long as it can provide a direct booking link or widget, we can build the site around it.

How long does it take?

A typical project takes around 3 weeks once we have everything we need from you.

We're not very technical. Is that a problem?

Not at all. Most of our clients aren't technical. We explain things in plain language and do the tech work for you.
