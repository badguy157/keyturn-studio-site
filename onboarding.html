How we'll get your new website live in about 3 weeks

Once you approve your quote, we follow a simple, step-by-step process. You'll always know what we're doing and what we need from you.

Step-by-step, start to finish

Step 1 – Confirm & schedule

You approve the quote, choose a start date that suits you, and pay the first instalment.

Step 2 – Access & kickoff

We ask for your domain registrar details (so we can point your web address to the new site), your booking link and the name of your booking system (no login needed), analytics (if you have it), and any photos or menus. Then we have a short kickoff call to agree priorities and make sure we understand your hotel.

Step 3 – Design & build (about 2 weeks)

We design and build your new website pages (home, rooms, food & drink, what's on, and key story/FAQ sections). You'll get a private link to review it.

Step 4 – Review & launch

You give feedback, we make refinements, and once you're happy, we launch the new site and keep an eye on it.

Share Your Access (Securely)

After you approve your quote, we'll need access to a few things so we can connect your new site properly. We use a secure link for any logins and never ask you to send passwords by email. We don't need login access to your existing website or to your Google listing – just the items below.

What we need (in 4 short steps)

  1. Analytics (if you have it). Add analytics@keyturn.studio as a Viewer in Google Analytics, or share read-only access to whatever you use.
  2. Domain / DNS. Access to your domain provider (for example GoDaddy, Namecheap or Cloudflare) so we can point your web address to the new site. You can use our secure link or delegate DNS-only access if you prefer not to share the main login.
  3. Booking engine. Your direct booking link and the name of your booking system (e.g. eviivo, SiteMinder). We don't need your booking system password—just the link and system name so we can wire up your "Book Now" buttons correctly.
  4. Brand assets. Logos, photos, menus and any style guidelines. You can upload these through the "Upload brand assets" link.

What happens after you send this

Once we've confirmed access, we'll book your project into our schedule and share your target launch week. We'll also suggest a short 20–30 minute kickoff call to walk through your priorities and make sure we understand your hotel.

If you're too busy for a call, we can handle the kickoff by email instead.

Tip: If you'd prefer to avoid cookie pop-ups, ask us about a simple privacy-friendly analytics option instead of Google Analytics.

Access & Setup Questions

No GA4 Yet?

We'll provision GA4 and Tag Manager for you.

Using Plausible/Fathom/Matomo?

Add read-only access; we'll wire key-click tracking.

How Do I Share DNS Safely?

Delegate DNS-only access or use our secure link.

What Counts As 'Booking Engine' Access?

Direct booking URL and engine name.

What If We Don't Have New Photos?

We'll launch with current assets and improve later.

When Is The Kickoff Call?

After deposit and access—sets timeline and responsibilities.

What we'll ask for

  • Domain / DNS access
    So we can connect the new site to your existing web address without fuss.
  • Booking system details
    So we can make sure all "Book Now" buttons go to the right place and work smoothly.
  • Analytics (optional)
    If you already use Google Analytics or another tool, we'll ask for read-only access so you can see how the new site is doing.
  • Photos & logo
    So we can show your rooms and spaces in the best light. If you don't have many photos, we'll discuss options.
  • Menus & key information (if applicable)
    So we can keep your food and drink pages up to date and accurate.

If you're not sure how to grant access to something, don't worry—we'll walk you through it step by step.

What we handle for you

  • Planning the layout of your new site
  • Writing and polishing the wording so it speaks to your ideal guests
  • Making sure the site looks good on phones, tablets and laptops
  • Setting up simple tracking so you can see how many people click "Book Now"
  • Checking that pages load quickly and are easy to use

You don't have to understand the technical details. That's our job.

After your new site goes live

  • We fix small issues that come up in the first 30 days
  • We show you how to request simple changes (text, photos, offers)
  • If you'd like, you can add an ongoing care plan so we keep an eye on performance and suggest improvements over time

Common questions

What if I can't find a password or login?

This happens all the time. We'll help you track down or reset what's needed.

Do we have to change booking systems?

Usually not. In most cases, we work with what you already use.

What if we're too busy during the project?

We designed the process so you only need to be involved at a few key moments: sending access, joining the kickoff call, and reviewing the draft site.
